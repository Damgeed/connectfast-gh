Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know about ordering data on ConnectFast

How do I receive the data after purchase? ▼
Data is delivered directly to your phone number within 30 seconds of payment confirmation. You'll receive an SMS from your network confirming the bundle has been activated.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), bank transfers, and PayPal. All payments are processed instantly.
Can I buy data for someone else? ▼
Absolutely! Just provide the recipient's phone number when ordering. We'll send the data directly to their line.
What if my data doesn't arrive? ▼
It's extremely rare, but if it happens, contact us on WhatsApp and we'll resolve it within minutes. We have a 100% delivery guarantee.
Do your plans auto-renew? ▼
No, none of our plans auto-renew. You only buy when you need it. Plans expire at the end of their validity period.
