Frequently asked

Everything you need to know

How do I receive data after purchase? ▼
Data is sent directly to your phone within 30 seconds of payment. You'll get an SMS from your network confirming activation.
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo Money), bank transfers, and PayPal. All processed instantly.
Can I buy data for someone else? ▼
Absolutely. Just provide the recipient's number when ordering. We'll deliver directly to their line.
What if my data doesn't arrive? ▼
Extremely rare, but if it happens, message us on WhatsApp and we'll resolve it within minutes. 100% delivery guarantee.
Do plans auto-renew? ▼
No. You only buy when you need it. All plans expire at the end of their validity period with no auto-renewal.
