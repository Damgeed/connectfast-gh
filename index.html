Frequently asked

Quick answers to common questions

How fast is the delivery? ▾
Most orders are delivered in under 30 seconds. If there's a delay, message us on WhatsApp and we'll sort it out immediately.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money). Just place your order and we'll send payment details on WhatsApp.
Do you sell airtime too? ▾
No — we only sell data bundles. Airtime is not available. Pure data, no fluff.
Can I buy for someone else's number? ▾
Yes! Just let us know the number you want the data sent to when you message us on WhatsApp. We'll load it on any MTN, Telecel, or AirtelTigo line.
